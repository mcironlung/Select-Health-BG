About Select Health of Bowling Green, KY

At Select Health, our professional staff is here to serve the community of Bowling Green, KY and surrounding areas with top notch health care.  Our staff is the best around.

Lena Gokey, APRN, NP-C

Lena Gokey, APRN, NP-C Lena began her nursing career in 2004 at Vanderbilt Children’s Hospital, where she worked as a neonatal nurse. After, working with neonates and new mothers, she began a career in Women’s Health as a labor and delivery nurse. Lena received her advance practice nursing degree from Western Kentucky University in 2012, with a focus in family health and wellness.

In addition, she has been teaching as a part-time clinical nursing instructor at Western Kentucky University for the past 10 years. In the spring of 2019, she completed specialized training in medical weight management and facial aesthetics from the International Association of Physicians in Aesthetic Medicine.

Over the last year, Lena has completed multiple advanced training courses in aesthetic medicine. Finally, in May of 2020, Lena made a long time dream a reality and opened Select Health LLC where she has combined total beauty with a new approach to health… medical + aesthetics + weight loss

Angie McDaniel, RN

Angie McDaniel, RN, a BG native began her nursing career in 2006 as a RN for Pediatric Associates in Bowling Green. She left the office setting and went to work at the Medical Center in the PACU (recovery room) and the cardiac cath lab. She decided to leave the hospital for her current position as a district nurse for Warren County School District.

While she loves her job in the school system, aesthetics has been something she has wanted to pursue for a long time. Angie states, “she is so excited for this opportunity and can’t wait to see where this takes her”.

Rachel Gilpin, RN, MSN,CRT

Native of Butler County, Rachel grew up on a farm while attending SKYCTC to obtain her Respiratory Therapy Certification and an Associate's Degree in Applied Science from Western Kentucky University. Rachel went on to serve her community as a nurse and nurse leader in Southern/Western KY and areas in Northern/Western TN over the next 20+ years. Graduating from Western KY University with an Associates, Bachelor's and Master's Degree in Nursing, Rachel uses her skills in areas of critical care, emergency care, trauma/burn, infectious disease, post-acute rehabilitation, medical-surgical, and other various service lines in the pediatric and adult enterprise.

Over the last year, Rachel has complemented her experience by adding training and certifications in aesthetics, medical weight loss, and IV hydration therapies. In 2021, Rachel joined Lena in a lifetime dream of helping people achieve a healthier & happier you from the inside-out!
