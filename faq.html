Frequently Asked Questions

Does Select Health accept insurance?

No, this frees us from contractual agreements that prevent healthcare providers in a traditional practice from offering wholesale prices on office visits and laboratory tests.

Does Select Health replace insurance?

We do recommend all patients have and maintain insurance coverage for medical catastrophes such as trauma, cancer, specialty providers, and unexpected surgery. We recommended you opt for a lower cost, higher deductible plan that would still offer you coverage, but would lower your monthly premium.

What if I do not have insurance?

Select Health will offer all the same benefits and care to you as with all of our patients.

What if I need care on the weekend?

If you have an urgent after-hours concern that you believe cannot wait for routine business hours, please go to your nearest walk-in care or emergency room.

What kind of copayment will I have to pay for office visits?

Zero. Your monthly membership covers the costs of office visits as often as needed. We only charge an additional fee if we provide discounted labs. However, your membership includes a yearly well exam with routine labs. We will always tell you ahead of time what any additional charges you would be responsible for, knowing what a medical service costs right up front and not 4-6 months later.

If I have Medicare, can I still join Select Health?

Yes, you will need to sign a one-time waiver declaring that neither you nor your doctor will directly bill Medicare for our services. Medicare will still cover laboratory testing, imaging, medication, or hospitalization ordered by our office.

Will I benefit from Select Health if I don’t require frequent medical attention?

Yes! While we always provide care for our sick patients, it is important to be proactive and create plans for staying healthy. Certainly, everyone benefits from healthcare tailored to them. You may also, pay per visit in lieu of a monthly membership fee. The per visit fee is $65, this does not include the annual wellness exam with labs.  The annual wellness exam with labs is $95 to $150, depending on what labs and testing are needed.

What is not available at Select Health?

We do not manage chronic pain and do not prescribe chronic pain medication. We do not have or keep any pain medication at the clinic. This is because these medications are heavily regulated, need substantial monitoring, have a high rate of diversion and for most patients, long term, is detrimental to their overall health.

Can I submit charges to my insurance company?

No

Can I get reimbursed for Select Health membership fees from my HSA or FSA?

Not at this time. Hopefully, in the near future.

Is my membership fee tax-deductible?

Please see a tax accountant, for consultation and/or legal advice with regards to your membership fees being tax-deductible.
