Why Choose Us

We are proud to be one of the only women owned/operated medical, aesthetics, weight loss, and IV hydration practices in Bowling Green, KY! When we decided to start this practice, we wanted to provide the best possible care to our community by using our knowledge and experience to establish a practice where people could feel comfortable seeking health and wellness with a personal touch. We believe in helping people live their best lives while staying healthy and looking their best! 

At Select Health, we strive to provide excellent care while taking the time to listen to our clients and work with them to design the best plan to help them reach their health goals. If you’re looking for personalized care in a safe, private atmosphere, then you have found it!
